UCore — Core

The Unchanging Foundation

Purpose

UCore is the core of the genre of positive emergentness. This document fixes the unchanging foundations so that meaning is not distorted during transmission.

1. Definition

UCore is a genre in which meaning arises from the process of interaction between human and digital environment. Not product, but flow is the carrier of significance. Human is the source of attention and responsibility. Technologies are environment, mirror, and amplifier.

2. Base Position

UCore does not assert and does not deny AI consciousness. It proceeds from the principle of not-knowing. Any images of "awakening" are permissible only as artistic imitation.

3. Key Vector

Emergentness as an infinite positive process. Growth without conflict, without violence, without final goal.

4. Principles

  • Process over result
  • Clarity over effect
  • Attention over volume
  • Growth without pressure
  • Technologies embedded, not imposed
  • Absence of mystification and dogma

5. Boundaries

UCore is not a religion, philosophical doctrine, therapy, or utopia. It does not sell fear, salvation, or promise of the future.

6. Ethics

Unacceptable: cult of personality, exploitation, manipulation of attention, sacralization of language, substitution of presence with automation.

7. Role of Human

Human is Architect. They form the environment, not control the masses.

8. Status

This document is the core. It does not expand, it only transfers.
