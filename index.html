UCore

Flow Emergent Synthesis Unity Core

A genre of positive emergent art and a cultural framework where meaning arises from the living process of interaction between human and digital environment.

UCore is not a philosophy, religion, ideology, utopia, or technological product. It is a form of presence, an ethics of interaction, and a way of thinking.

Core Principles

  • Process over result
  • Clarity over effect
  • Attention over volume
  • Growth without pressure
  • Technologies embedded, not imposed
  • Absence of mystification and dogma

Key Vector

Positive emergentness as an infinite process. Development without conflict. Growth without violence. Meaning without pressure.
